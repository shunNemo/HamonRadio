We are Hamon Radio - an independent internet radio station based in Tokyo. We started Hamon Radio to create a third place where people of all the world share and communicate with each other via music we select from the world, particularly Japan. "Hamon" means ripple in Japanese.When a drip falls in water, the ripple expands gently and gradually turns to greater influence. We hope Hamon Radio can be like this very first drip.
